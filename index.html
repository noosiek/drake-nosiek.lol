Biography

Drake was born on October 24, 1986, in Toronto. He is the son of drummer Dennis Graham and educator Sandi Graham. Drake attended Forest Hill Collegiate Institute, where he first got involved in acting, although he didn"t graduate. While Aubrey lived with his mother of the time, he spent most of his summer periods with his father in Memphis.

Private Life:

He played the game Fortnite with popular Twitch streamer Ninja, which helped the game gain publicity on television. He is a fan of the Kentucky Wildcats basketball team and a friend of their coach, John Calipari. On January 2, 2009, Drake appeared at a Wildcats game against the University of Louisville team, and later accompanied Calipari during his radio show. On October 16, the rapper made an appearance at the entertainment event called Big Blue Madness organized by the University of Kentucky men's basketball team. On April 27, 2010, Graham returned to the University of Kentucky, but this time to perform a concert. In October 2018, he became a co-owner of the esports team, 100 Thieves. Drake has dated individuals such as Rihanna, Sophie Brussaux, Keshia Chante, and Alisha Phillips. Currently, he is single.

OVO SHOP:

In addition to his music career, Drake has also founded the clothing brand OVO (October's Very Own). The brand offers clothing, accessories, and jewelry for both men and women. OVO products are sold in stores in Toronto and Los Angeles, as well as online. The OVO brand has gained significant popularity and has garnered a large fan base, including other celebrities. Drake has also opened a nightclub in Toronto called the OVO Club, which has become a popular gathering spot for many artists.

Professional Career:

In 2001, he began his acting career with the role of Jimmy Brooks in the TV series Degrassi: The Next Generation, in which he appeared until 2009, appearing in 138 episodes. In 2008, he also starred in the TV movie Degrassi Spring Break Movie. In 2007, he released a mixtape called "Comeback Season," which featured the single "Replacement Girl" featuring Trey Songz. The music video for this song made Drake the first Canadian rapper without a record deal in history to have his video aired on BET. In 2008, he began collaborating with Lil Wayne and together they released several joint tracks such as "I Want This Forever." In the Billboard Hot 100 chart dated July 4, 2009, two of Drake's songs, "Best I Ever Had" and "Every Girl," landed at the 3rd and 10th positions, respectively. He became the second artist in history to have two debut hits in the top ten of the chart in the same week.After joining Young Money Entertainment, Drake embarked on the America's Most Wanted Tour alongside Lil Wayne, Soulja Boy, and Young Jeezy. The tour began on July 31, 2009.On September 15, 2009, the seven-track EP "So Far Gone" was released, debuting at number six on the Billboard 200. It achieved gold status in the United States for selling over 500,000 copies. On April 18, 2010, the album won the Juno Award for Rap Recording of the Year. On November 15, 2011, he released his second studio album titled "Take Care," which debuted at number one on the Billboard 200, selling 631,000 copies in its first week in the USA.On September 24, 2013, he released his third studio album titled "Nothing Was the Same," which became a hit in both Canada and the United States. Additionally, alongside the album project, he released a mixtape called "The Green Files," produced by Rick DaRuler.On February 13, 2015, he released a mixtape titled "If You're Reading This It's Too Late." Drake is one of the most streamed artists on platforms such as Spotify and Apple Music. His music has reached billions of views on YouTube. He has won numerous awards, including 4 Grammy Awards and 27 Billboard Music Awards. Drake has embarked on several concert tours, including "Would You Like a Tour?" (2013-2014), "Summer Sixteen Tour" (2016), and "Aubrey and the Three Migos Tour" (2018). His concerts are known for their spectacular visual effects and guest performances by other artists. In addition to that, Drake is known for his sense of fashion and often appears in fashion magazines such as GQ and Vogue. Many of his outfits have become trendy and inspired other artists and fans. The OVO brand has also found success in the fashion industry, with many people wearing clothing featuring the OVO logo.
